## Outreach Strategy for ReadIn AI

### Week 1-2: Accelerators
1. Apply to Y Combinator Spring 2026 (PRIORITY)
2. Apply to Techstars programs
3. Apply to Antler and Boost VC

### Week 3-4: AI-Focused VCs
1. Email Tier 2 investors (AI-focused)
2. Customize pitch for AI-native positioning
3. Emphasize stealth mode differentiation

### Week 5-6: SaaS/Productivity VCs
1. Email Tier 3 investors
2. Emphasize productivity/meeting tools angle
3. Highlight enterprise potential

### Week 7-8: Generalist VCs
1. Email Tier 4 investors
2. Broader market opportunity pitch
3. Follow up on all previous outreach

---

## Key Pitch Points for Investors

1. **AI-Native**: Built with AI at the core, not bolted on
2. **Stealth Mode**: Unique differentiator - completely invisible to other participants
3. **Large TAM**: 1B+ professionals in video calls daily
4. **Multiple Revenue Streams**: Individual ($30/mo) + Team ($20/user) + Enterprise
5. **Production Ready**: Fully built, deployed at www.getreadin.us
6. **Multi-Platform**: Windows, macOS, Linux, Browser Extensions
7. **Privacy-First**: On-device processing, GDPR compliant
