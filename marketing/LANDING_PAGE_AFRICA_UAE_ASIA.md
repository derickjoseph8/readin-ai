## 11. FAQ Section

```
## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is ReadIn AI available in my country?
Yes! ReadIn AI is available worldwide. We offer localized pricing for
Africa, UAE, and Asia regions, with local payment methods including
M-Pesa, Paystack, and Flutterwave.

### How does the free trial work?
Your 7-day trial gives you full access to all features. No credit card
required to start. When your trial ends, you can subscribe or your
account simply becomes inactive (no charges).

### What languages are supported?
ReadIn AI supports 45 languages including English, French, Arabic, Swahili,
Hindi, Mandarin, and many more. The AI can provide talking points in the
same language as your conversation.

### Is my conversation data private?
Absolutely. ReadIn AI processes everything locally on your device. Your
audio and conversations are never recorded, stored, or sent to external
servers. Privacy is built into our core architecture.

### Will others in the meeting know I'm using it?
No. ReadIn AI runs silently in the background. There's no bot joining
your call, no recording indicator, nothing visible to other participants.

### What meeting apps does it work with?
ReadIn AI works with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Webex, Slack
Huddles, Discord, and 35+ other apps. If you can have a meeting on it,
we likely support it.

### How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT requires you to type questions and wait for answers. ReadIn AI
listens to your live conversation and provides relevant talking points
in real-time - no typing, no waiting, no context-switching.

### Can I pay with M-Pesa or mobile money?
Yes! We support M-Pesa (Kenya), Paystack (Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa,
Kenya), and Flutterwave across Africa. Enterprise customers can also
pay via bank transfer.

### Is there a team or company plan?
Yes. Our Team plan ($15.99/user/month) is perfect for 10-50 users. For
larger organizations (50+), our Enterprise plan includes custom pricing,
SSO, and dedicated support. Contact sales to learn more.

### What if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If ReadIn AI isn't right for
you, contact us within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.
```
