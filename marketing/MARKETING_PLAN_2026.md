# ReadIn AI - Marketing Plan 2026

## Executive Summary

ReadIn AI is a real-time AI meeting assistant that provides instant talking points during live conversations. This document outlines region-specific marketing strategies for:
- **Plan A**: Europe & Americas
- **Plan B**: Africa (Kenya, South Africa, Ghana, Nigeria + emerging markets)

**Product Pricing:**
- Free Trial: 7 days, no credit card
- Premium: $29.99/month or $299.90/year (save $60)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing
