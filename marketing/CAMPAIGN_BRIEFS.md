## Campaign 1: Nigeria Launch Campaign

### Campaign Name: "Sound Brilliant Lagos"

### Objective
Launch ReadIn AI in Nigeria with 2,000 trial signups and 160 paid conversions in 90 days.

### Target Audience

**Primary Segments**:
1. **Fintech Professionals** (30%)
   - Age: 25-40
   - Role: Product managers, sales, business development
   - Companies: Paystack, Flutterwave, Kuda, OPay, PalmPay
   - Pain: Pitching to investors, client meetings

2. **Banking & Finance** (25%)
   - Age: 28-45
   - Role: Relationship managers, corporate bankers
   - Companies: GTBank, Access Bank, Zenith, FirstBank
   - Pain: Client advisory, regulatory knowledge

3. **Tech Startup Founders** (20%)
   - Age: 22-35
   - Role: Founders, co-founders, early employees
   - Location: Yaba, Lekki, VI
   - Pain: Investor pitches, partnership meetings

4. **Consultants & Professional Services** (15%)
   - Age: 26-40
   - Role: Consultants, analysts
   - Companies: McKinsey Lagos, Deloitte, PwC, KPMG
   - Pain: Client presentations, expertise demonstration

5. **Sales Professionals** (10%)
   - Age: 24-38
   - Role: B2B sales, account executives
   - Industries: SaaS, Enterprise software
   - Pain: Objection handling, product knowledge

### Key Messages

**Primary Message**:
"Sound like the smartest person in every meeting. ReadIn AI gives you real-time talking points powered by AI."

**Supporting Messages**:
- "Never freeze in an investor pitch again"
- "Close more deals with AI-powered confidence"
- "Your secret weapon for high-stakes meetings"
- "Used by top professionals across Lagos"

### Channels & Tactics

#### Digital (70% of budget)

**Twitter/X Campaign** (30% of digital)
- Partner with Nigerian tech Twitter influencers
- Sponsored tweets during peak hours (8-10am, 12-2pm, 7-9pm WAT)
- Hashtags: #NigeriaTech #LagosStartups #SoundBrilliant
- Thread content: "How I aced my Sequoia pitch using AI"

**LinkedIn Campaign** (25% of digital)
- Sponsored content targeting job titles
- InMail to decision makers at target companies
- Company page content in English

**Instagram/TikTok** (20% of digital)
- Short-form video content
- "Day in the life" of professionals using ReadIn AI
- Relatable meeting humor content

**Google Ads** (15% of digital)
- Keywords: "AI meeting assistant Nigeria", "pitch deck helper"
- Remarketing to website visitors

**WhatsApp Marketing** (10% of digital)
- Build community group (cap at 256, create multiple)
- Share tips, product updates, success stories
- Customer support channel

#### Influencer Marketing (20% of budget)

**Tier 1 Influencers** (2-3 people, $2,000-5,000 each):
- Tech Twitter personalities with 50K+ followers
- LinkedIn Top Voices in Nigeria
- Deliverable: 3 posts + 1 video review

**Tier 2 Influencers** (5-8 people, $500-1,500 each):
- Niche professionals (sales coaches, career advisors)
- Deliverable: 2 posts + story mentions

**Tier 3 Micro-influencers** (15-20 people, $100-300 each):
- Startup founders, tech employees
- Deliverable: Authentic testimonial posts

#### Events & Partnerships (10% of budget)

**Tech Hub Partnerships**:
- CcHub Lagos: Workshop sponsorship
- Ventures Platform: Demo day presence
- Zone Tech Park: Monthly meetup sponsor

**Events**:
- Lagos Startup Week (booth + speaking slot)
- Fintech Association Nigeria events
- MBA networking events (LBS, Pan-Atlantic)

### Budget Breakdown

**Total Budget**: $35,000 (90 days)

| Category | Amount | % |
|----------|--------|---|
| Twitter/X Ads | $7,350 | 21% |
| LinkedIn Ads | $6,125 | 17.5% |
| Instagram/TikTok | $4,900 | 14% |
| Google Ads | $3,675 | 10.5% |
| WhatsApp Marketing | $2,450 | 7% |
| Tier 1 Influencers | $4,000 | 11.4% |
| Tier 2 Influencers | $2,000 | 5.7% |
| Tier 3 Influencers | $1,000 | 2.9% |
| Events & Partnerships | $3,500 | 10% |

### Timeline

**Week 1-2: Pre-Launch**
- Influencer outreach and contracts
- Content creation
- Landing page localization
- WhatsApp group setup

**Week 3-4: Soft Launch**
- Invite-only access for influencers
- Gather initial testimonials
- Beta user feedback
- Refine messaging

**Week 5-8: Public Launch**
- Full campaign activation
- Influencer posts go live
- Paid ads at full budget
- PR push to TechCabal, Techpoint

**Week 9-12: Optimization**
- Scale winning channels
- Cut underperformers
- Focus on conversion
- Retargeting campaigns
